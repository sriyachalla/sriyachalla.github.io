Product Studio

What is Product Studio? Product Studio at Cornell Tech is an innovative, semester-long course that serves as a cornerstone for product development education. It's designed for students to work in teams to address real "How Might We" questions from companies, providing hands-on experience with the full product development cycle. This includes identifying key problems, ideation, design, the development process, and constructing a meaningful narrative around the product. The course is highly dynamic, with students presenting their progress at various stages, culminating in a final product presentation
